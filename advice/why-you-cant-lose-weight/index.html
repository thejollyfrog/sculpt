Why you can't lose weight

Written on January 1, 0001

Most of us like to be in good shape. However, few people are able to reach their ideal or even come close.

This is because we have been taught to think about food wrongly.

There are, likely, two main problems with your diet:

  • You are addicted to certain foods
  • Many of the foods you need to satisfy your body (and avoid deficiency) are missing or in short supply

There is no way to exercise away a poor diet. Both poor diet and exercise cause stress to your body and to master them, you must first understand how they impact your body.

What can you do differently?

By consuming only highly nutritious healthy foods and herbs, you will be able to not only master your diet, but also conquer your health and rejuvenate yourself in the process.

Exercise should be frequent, but not overly stressing to the body. Training for very long periods or doing too much high impact aerobic exercise will hinder you — causing damage to your joints and causing age acceleration.

Instead, I can show you exercises which you can practice, without a gym, which will challenge you and transform your physique.
