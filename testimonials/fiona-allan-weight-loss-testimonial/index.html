Fiona Allan Weight Loss Testimonial

“Over seven months I lost five stone, gained fitness and muscle tone, and totally transformed the way I think about food and eating.” — Fiona Allan, CEO, Birmingham Hippodrome

Mike Kirk changed my life. When I met him, I was an extremely unhealthy weight, totally unfit, and ate and drank whatever I liked whenever I liked. Comfort eating, snacking all day, alcohol every night, coffee to wake me up the next day: a cycle that had gone on for years.

I’d had enough, but didn’t know how to change my habits or whether I had what it would take to stick with it. Mike took things slowly, to start with, getting me used to the idea of exercise, healthier eating and slow weight loss over a period of time.

He motivated and encouraged me and gave me the confidence to believe that I could achieve my goals. Over seven months I lost five stone, gained fitness and muscle tone, and totally transformed the way I think about food and eating.

My life is different now. I am healthy, motivated, enjoy exercise and healthy eating, and will never return to my old ways. My only regret is that I left it so long to get started. Mike is not just a brilliant trainer but a fantastic motivational coach. He has also become a great friend.

Fiona Allan

CEO, Birmingham Hippodrome
